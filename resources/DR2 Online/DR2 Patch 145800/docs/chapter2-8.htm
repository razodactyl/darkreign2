2.8     Trails Menu (8 key)

Used for creating AI paths (trails).

To create a trail:

All trails must be set to a team. First, highlight the team in the Teams menu that the trail will belong to. Select "One Way", "Two Way" or "Looping". Left click the terrain to begin laying the trail points. Right click to erase trail points. When your trail is finished, type a name for it in the rectangular text box. Then click "Create".
To edit an existing trail, highlight the trail on the list. You can now right click to remove points and re-draw the trail.
Trails are referred to in the game script.

See Section 5.10 for more info on using trails with AI and scripting.
